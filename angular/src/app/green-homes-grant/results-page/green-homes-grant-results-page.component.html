What can I use the funding for?

You can use the Green Homes Grant to help fund the installation of energy efficiency and low carbon heating improvements to your property.

You can find out which measures are best suited to your situation by answering a few additional questions about your property.

Alternatively, you can find the full list of measures covered under the Green Homes Grant by going to the Green Homes Grant page.
