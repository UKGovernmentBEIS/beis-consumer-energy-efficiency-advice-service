Energy Company Obligation
Grants for energy efficiency home improvements
The Energy Companies Obligation (ECO) could help you get financial support from your supplier with insulation, replacing or repairing your boiler, or other upgrades to your heating.

Renewable Heat Incentive
The Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI) is a scheme designed to financially reward you if you use renewable energy to heat your home.

Feed-In Tariff
The feed-in tariff (FIT) scheme offers guaranteed cash payments to households that produce their own electricity using renewable technologies, such as solar photovoltaic (PV) panels or wind turbines.
See if you could get paid for generating energy
Winter Fuel Payments
If you were born on or before 5 August 1953 you could get between £100 and £300 to help you pay your heating bills
Warm Home Discount
If you're receiving certain benefits, you could get £140 off your electricity bill for winter 2017 to 2018
Cold Weather Payments
If you're receiving certain benefits, you may get a payment when the weather is cold
