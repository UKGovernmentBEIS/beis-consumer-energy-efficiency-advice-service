Green Homes Grant

The Chancellor announced on 8th July a £2bn Green Homes Grant in England.

Under this scheme homeowners and landlords will be able to apply for a voucher to fund up to two thirds of the cost of hiring tradespeople to upgrade the energy performance of their home (up to a maximum contribution of around £5000). Low income households will be eligible for up to 100% government funding, up to around £10,000. Further details on the range of measures available and how the scheme will operate will be announced before the scheme’s full launch in the Autumn.
