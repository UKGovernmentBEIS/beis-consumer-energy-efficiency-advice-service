Home energy grants
Energy Company Obligation
Grants for energy efficiency home improvements
You might be able to get help for energy-saving improvements to your home if you live in social housing, or if you live in private housing and claim certain benefits. You may get help with the cost of insulation work, replacing or repairing your boiler, or other upgrades to your heating.
Renewable Heat Incentive
Get cash if you install or have already installed an eligible renewable heating technology.
Feed-In Tariff
Get paid for creating your own green energy.
Winter Fuel Payments
If you were born on or before 5 August 1953 you could get between £100 and £300 to help you pay your heating bills.
Warm Home Discount
If you're receiving certain benefits, you could get £140 off your electricity bill for winter 2017 to 2018.
Cold Weather Payments
If you're receiving certain benefits, you may get a payment when the weather is cold.
