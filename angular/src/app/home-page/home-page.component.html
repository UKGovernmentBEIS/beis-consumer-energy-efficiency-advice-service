Boiler Upgrade Scheme

Grants available to support the installation of a low carbon heating technology your home

The Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) is a government scheme to support the decarbonisation of homes and small non-domestic buildings in England and Wales. The scheme will provide upfront capital grants of £5000 or £6000 to encourage property owners to replace existing fossil fuel heating with more efficient, low carbon heating systems including heat pumps and biomass boilers.
